**Working title: Star Damage**

You're the manager of an AI rockstar. He's a fully autonomous agent living on a real 24-hour clock — texting you, sending voice messages, and calling you through push notifications. He doesn't pause when you close the app. Miss a call and he ends up in jail. Ignore his texts and he spirals.

**Your goal:** Get him from nobody to superstar — book gigs, manage his image, handle crises, negotiate contracts — all while he keeps making terrible life decisions.

## Core Loop

- The rockstar lives in real-time on your actual clock
- He contacts you with problems, opportunities, and chaos (texts, voice messages, calls)
- You can also reach out to him anytime
- Your management decisions shape his career trajectory and personal life
- His actions depend on who he meets, his current state, and your guidance

## The Chaos

- Drug problems that escalate if ignored
- A maniac girlfriend who causes drama
- Contracts STDs
- Gets into situations requiring ambulances or police
- News articles about his antics
- Career opportunities he might sabotage

## Key Features

- **Real-time 24hr cycle** — he lives on your clock, not game time
- **Two-way communication** — he activates you, you activate him
- **Push notifications** — browser notifications pull you into crises
- **Voice messages and calls** — in-browser voice experience
- **Archetype selection** — choose from different rockstar personalities (diva, punk, quiet genius, etc.)
- **Career progression** — guide them from open mics to sold-out arenas
- **Satirical tone** — over-the-top celebrity drama, reality TV energy

## Platform

Mobile web game.

## V1 Scope

One playable archetype, full text + voice experience, real-time notifications, roughly two weeks of AI-driven story that branches based on management decisions. Proving the "living AI character" concept.

## Concepts

- **God Agents** — the rockstar is an autonomous AI agent observing state and making decisions
- **Personality Games** — player reads and adapts to the rockstar's distinct personality
- **Resonance Engine** — emotional dynamics between player and rockstar drive outcomes
